The Problem

Lost items rarely
find their way home

Every day, thousands of people lose personal items — wallets, phones, keys, pets — with no effective centralized way to report or find them. Traditional methods are fragmented, inefficient, and frustrating.

✕

No central platform to post or search lost items

✕

Privacy concerns about sharing contact information publicly

✕

Outdated listings that clog searches and waste time

Our Solution

A smart, secure &
community platform

Lost & Found brings the community together with real-time posts, intelligent search, anonymous contact, and automatic status management — making reunions happen faster.

✓

Centralized real-time database powered by Firebase

✓

Anonymous contact system protects your privacy

✓

Automatic status tracking and expiry management

Features

Everything you need

Built with the community in mind, every feature is designed to maximize reunions.

📋

Found Item Posts

Report items you've found with photos, location, and details so the rightful owner can reclaim them.

🎫

Lost Item Tickets

Search & Filter
